Enjoy multiplayer live-action gaming with anyone, anywhere, anytime - All powered by a phone you already own

Happy Trigger simplifies all the fun of laser tag to an app! Our technology uses your phone's camera to understand your environment, so all you need is a special shirt to play!
Once all the players have downloaded the app you can join or host a new game & play we up to three players
Be sure to pick the team that matches your HT shirt. Unlike regular laser tag, HT works best is bright areas (More teams & colors coming soon!)
Pick a safe, well lit area, indoors or outdoors, & find a good spot to start away from the other team. Big backyards & public parks with places to hide are perfect!
Once the game starts use your phone's screen to aim at other players. Earn points by lining up your opponent's shirt within the target circle.
The first players to reach the score limit wins the round! Change locations and score limit to mix up the fun! Plenty of futuristic features and accessories that take the game to the next level are on the way! Happy Trigger is lasertag with anyone, anywhere, anytime. Play now!
